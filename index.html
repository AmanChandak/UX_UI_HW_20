Infinity - The Post Pandemic Mobile Travel App

This case study was created to understand how COVID-19 has affected individuals who have travelled, or plan to travel in the future, while giving them a resource that allows them to travel safely and well informed.

THE PROBLEM

Individuals need help planning their post-pandemic travels, while knowing it is safe and accessible due to the constant change in COVID-19 restrictions and regulations around the world.

THE SOLUTION

A mobile travel application that is designed for post-pandemic travel, which will help individuals safely plan their trip while being educated on the COVID-19 restrictions and guidelines around the world. It will provide the cheapest rates, while offering transportation, accommodation and activities, all in one place.

CONCLUSIONS / FINAL THOUGHTS

  • FINDING THE MVP
    I found myself losing focus on the main goal of this project as I went through the phases, which was safety. I had the idea that Infinity would be an application that focused on COVID-19 safety and I spent too much time on all the other features instead of that one. It wasn’t until I defined the minimum viable product of Infinity that I was able to start making any progress. I had to go back and iterate a lot of my work from the previous phases but I learned that that is all part of the design process. Better Competitive Analysis I realized later on that a lot of the existing travel apps are adapting to the COVID-19 safety structure , which I thought wasn’t the case and would make Infinity one of a kind. I ended up pulling a lot of inspiration from Expedia to create my app, and built the structure of Infinity around that. New Discoveries I was amazed how fast an idea turned into a prototype even after knowing very little about UX design. I felt like I was making mistake after mistake and there were times where I thought I couldn’t fix it. However, after much guidance from teachers and peers I was able to put together a project I am happy with, and I feel like the challenge was all worth it. I have learned a lot and I am excited to move forward, make more mistakes, and continue the learning process. Finale I have learned how to think differently and creatively throughout this project, and it will help me improve my design process in future projects.
